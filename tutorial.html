~Tutorial & Rules~

-You are an explorer trying to find long lost treasure hidden miles underground in a dark cave. Rumor has it the treasure is surrounded by deadly traps. You decide to lower yourself from above at fixed coordinates.

-Search a 2D grid until you find the treasure or run out of health points.

-Click a space on the grid to guess where the treasure is hidden.

-"â˜»" marks where you are currently standing on the grid.

-"?" represents unexplored territory. You might find the treasure...or trigger a mine instead!

-"[ ]" represents an empty space. These spaces hold neither treasure nor trap, but they lower the number of spaces you must check in each level.

-"[#]" represents a lantern. Find one on a space and it will reveal up to 4 neighboring spaces.

-"*" This is a mine. It releases toxic gas when stepped on, killing you slowly. You will lose some HP if you land on a mine.

-[$] the treasure is hidden in a random "?" space. Find it in three levels in a row to beat the game!

-Click 'Quit Game' to stop playing and return to the menu.

-Scoring-

EASY Mode: 500 + (Health x2) per level

MEDIUM Mode: 600 + (Health x2.5) per level

HARD Mode: 800 + (Health x3.5) per level
